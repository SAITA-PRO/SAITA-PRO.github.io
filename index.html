Understanding DeFi
Decentralized finance is a blockchain-based financial system that recreates traditional financial services such as lending, borrowing, trading, and asset management without the need for intermediaries like banks. Key features include:

Smart Contracts: Self-executing smart contracts automate and enforce the terms of agreements without intermediaries, providing trustless transactions.

Liquidity Pools: Users contribute assets to decentralized liquidity pools, facilitating decentralized trading, lending, and borrowing.

Tokenization: Assets are represented as tokens on the blockchain, enabling fractional ownership, easy transferability, and increased liquidity.
